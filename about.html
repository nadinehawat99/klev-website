About Us

Klev, a Lebanese-based company initiated in 2019, mainly focuses on development and consultancy.

Our foremost objective is to provide our clients with a wide variety of services, assisting you in accelerating growth and promoting innovation. Klev’s passionate team members are always at your service, ready to take your tech experience to another level in web and mobile app development, project management, consultancy and web security.

With technology, there are no limitations; and at Klev, our passion has no limits.

Our Story

Klev’s Leaf Clover consists of 4 main parts; each section contains a specific value that we consider as a duty towards our customers.

We always do whatever it takes to provide quality and excellent service, taking into consideration international standards delivered with ethics and integrity; as for our team, honesty is the best policy.

At Klev, we are not afraid to do things differently, thus we never miss the opportunity to be innovative and people-oriented, focusing on various cultures and relationships in our developmental work and consultancy aid.
